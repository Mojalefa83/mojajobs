INTERVIEW TIPS

1. How did you hear about this position?

2. What do you know about our company?

3. What do you hope to gain from this position?

4. Where do you see yourself in five years?

5. Why did you leave your last job?

6. What is your dream job?

7. What do you enjoy as a recreational activity?

8. What is one word your former coworkers would use to describe you?

9. Tell me about a time you overcame a challenge at a previous job.

10. What is your proudest professional accomplishment?
